## Features
- Seamless Video Capture Snap high-quality video frames directly from any YouTube video straight into your workspace without interrupting your viewing experience.
- Advanced Image Annotation Take full control over your captured frames with a built-in image cropper and highlighter tools that let you mark up specific areas of interest instantly.
- Smart Text Extraction (OCR) Automatically extract text directly from your video screenshots using fully local Optical Character Recognition, making it effortless to copy code or presentation slides.
- Rich Document Editor Format your study materials with a powerful WYSIWYG editor supporting custom text colors, tables, dynamic block styles, lists, math equations, and custom fonts.
- Ultimate Organization Keep your knowledge base pristine with a dedicated Library view featuring advanced search capabilities, grouping, tagging, recent history, and custom sorting options.
- Total Privacy & Local Storage Your data never leaves your computer. Every keystroke is auto-saved directly to your browser's local IndexedDB storage, ensuring a fast, offline, and 100% private experience with zero tracking.
- Universal Export Options Take your notes anywhere by exporting them instantly to PDF, Microsoft Word (.doc), Markdown, or Plain Text formats.
- Secure Backup & Restore Safeguard your entire note library by downloading a complete JSON backup of your workspace, allowing you to easily restore your data across different browsers or devices.

## Installation (Developer Mode)
1. Download or clone this repository.
2. Go to `chrome://extensions/` in Google Chrome.
3. Enable **Developer mode** in the top right corner.
4. Click **Load unpacked** and select this directory.
5. Open a YouTube video and click the **StudySnap Pro** button to start taking notes!
